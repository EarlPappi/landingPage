Chris Mills

HEAVY METAL CODING

In this unconventional talk, Chris Mills will encourage you to pick up some drum sticks in addition to your laptop, and beat out a rythm of learning - both of code and drum beats! You'll learn the basics of JavaScript and CSS, plus the beginner's heavy metal drum patterns. The two are a marriage made in heaven, as nothing is a better antidote to code frustration than beating seven shades of hell out of a drum kit.
